Frequently Asked Questions

We have answered your questions.

Are my entries safe? +
Yes, absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and isolated database architecture. Your journal entries are processed completely anonymously and are never shared with third parties.
How does the AI analysis work? +
Our system uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze underlying emotional patterns (Joy, Anger, Sadness, etc.) in your sentences. It then provides personalized music, activity, and color suggestions inspired by clinical approaches like the ISO principle.
Is the app free? +
Journaling, basic emotion analysis, and the smart recommendation engine will always remain free. We believe mental health is a fundamental right, not a luxury.
